Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services and working process.

How long does a typical website project take?
The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A simple brochure website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex e-commerce or web applications can take 4-8 weeks. We'll establish a clear timeline during the planning phase.
Do you offer revisions on your work?
Yes, I include a specific number of revision rounds in each project package. For website projects, this typically includes 2-3 rounds of revisions. For writing and narration services, we agree on the revision policy upfront.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept various payment methods including bank transfers, PayPal, and digital payment platforms. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion, though this can be adjusted based on project size and duration.
Can you work with clients internationally?
Absolutely! I work with clients from all around the world. Communication is primarily done via email, video calls, and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.
